Driving Directions

From Salisbury

Take Route 13 South past Princess Anne. Exit on Route 413 South to Crisfield. Follow Route 413 approximately 15 miles into the City of Crisfield.

The Chamber of Commerce is on the right four blocks before the end of Route 413 near the intersection of 9th Street. The Chamber office is next to the Crisfield Times office.

From the South and East - Eastern Shore of Virginia

Follow Route 13 North past Pocomoke City. Turn left onto Route 667. The sign will say to Smith and Tangier Island. Follow Rt. 667 approximately 7 miles. The road will end at a stop sign and Route 667 turns left. Turn left and follow Route 667 another 4 miles until you come to a four-way-stop in the Town of Marion. Proceed through the four-way-stop and continue until you come to another stop sign at Route 413. Turn left onto Route 413 and follow approximately 7 miles into the City of Crisfield.

The Chamber of Commerce is on the right four blocks before the end of Route 413 near the intersection of 9th Street. The Chamber office is next to the Crisfield Times office.
