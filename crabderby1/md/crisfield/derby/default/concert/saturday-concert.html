Saturday September 5, 2015

Alter Ego

Alter Ego was formed in 2011 by bringing together the varying styles and influences of five of the area's most active musicians. Focusing on a diverse mix of covers, featuring the best of Rock, Pop and Dance, Alter Ego brings one of the most entertaining, energetic and polished acts to the stage each and every night in the Mid-Atlantic area.

The band features Jamie Sadler on vocals, John and Jim Boyer on guitar, Anthony Grabowski on drums, and Michael Vance on lead guitar and vocals.

Their song list includes all-time Pop, Rock and Classic favorites like Bad Company's Feel Like Making Love, Van Halen's You Really Got Me, and Black Eyed Peas's I Got a Feeling.

Always A Sell-Out Event

The City of Crisfield and the National Hard Crab Derby are no strangers to great concerts. Home of the former Tangier Sound Country Music Festival, the Crab Derby has hosted such acts as Faith Hill, Brooks and Dunn and Tim McGraw with nationally known cover bands like Little Texas.
