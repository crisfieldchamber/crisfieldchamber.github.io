44th Annual Crab Derby Boat Docking Contest
Sunday, September 6, 2015

Somers Cove Marina

To compete in the boat docking competition you must be a working waterman with a work boat. Register the morning of the event starting at 9 AM at Somers Cove Marina around B pier. Registration is free.

Prizes vary each year depending on sponsorship and how many participate.
